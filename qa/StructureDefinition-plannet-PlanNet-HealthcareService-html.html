PlanNet-HealthcareService Profile

The PlanNet-HealthCareService resource typically describes services offered by an organization/practitioner at a location. The resource may be used to encompass a variety of services covering the entire healthcare spectrum, including promotion, prevention, diagnstics, hospital and ambulatory care, home care, long-term care, and other health-related and community services.
