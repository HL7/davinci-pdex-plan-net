Definition:
A PlanNet-Network refers to a healthcare provider insurance network. A healthcare provider insurance network is an aggregation of organizations and individuals that deliver a set of services across a geography through health insurance products/plans. A network is typically owned by a payer.

In the PlanNet IG, individuals and organizations are represented as participants in a PLan-Net Network through the PlanNet-practitionerRole and Plan-Net-organizationAffiliation resources, respectively.
